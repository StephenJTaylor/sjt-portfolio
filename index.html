I'm currently Head of Post-Production at Fresh Cut Video, where I lead a team of editors delivering everything from brand social content to documentaries to long-form podcasts and interviews. Quality and attention to detail sits at the centre of everything we produce.

Over the years, I've worked as a producer/director and camera operator across a wide range of projects in the UK and internationally. Much of my work has focused on crafting strong narratives and translating complex ideas into engaging, accessible content for brands, charities and institutions.
